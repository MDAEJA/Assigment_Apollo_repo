Frequently asked questions

What is the Apollo 24|7 CIRCLE membership programme?
Apollo 24|7 CIRCLE membership is a one-of-its-kind membership designed to deliver the best care and value to its subscribers. Once you sign up with the plan, you will receive benefits such as priority access to doctors, cashback on medicines, free home delivery on a priority basis, and discounts on doctor consultations and lab tests.
How many plans available for CIRCLE membership?
There are 3 plans available for you to choose from: Rs 99 for 2 months, Rs 199 for 6 months, and Rs 299 for 12 months.
How do i sign up for CIRCLE membership?
It''s simple! If you''re a part of Apollo 24|7 already, you can click on the link above and sign up. If you''re new to Apollo 24|7, download the app today and get started.
Is annual membership better than monthly membership?
Yes, if you want to save big! The yearly membership only costs you Rs.299, which translates to Rs.25 per month.
Where will I get CIRCLE cashback?
You will be eligible for CIRCLE cashback every time you buy medicines, pharmacy products and Apollo products except when you have utilised certain specific coupons available on Apollo 24|7 for which CIRCLE cashback would not be applicable. The terms of such specific coupons shall supersede the CIRCLE cashback. We also have discounts on selected doctor consultations and diagnostic tests. Cashback will be credited to your OneApollo Health Credits as soon as a transaction gets completed.
How can I use CIRCLE cashback?
You can use your CIRCLE cashback as Health Credits to save on your pharmacy orders; and on diagnostic tests on Apollo 24|7.
Can I cancel CIRCLE membership after purchasing it?
CIRCLE membership is non-refundable, however you can contact us using the ''Need Help'' section in My Account and we will try our best to help you out.
Can the offers change after I buy the membership?
Yes, it’s possible but we always aim to upgrade benefits so that you get the best out of your CIRCLE membership.
What does CIRCLE cashback mean? Is it different from OneApollo cashback?
Every time you make using CIRCLE benefits, you will be rewarded with CIRCLE Cashback that you can use to purchase medicines and Apollo brand products. It''s the same as OneApollo Cashback.
Can CIRCLE cashback can be used for a virtual consultation?
Not yet, but you can use your CIRCLE cashback as Health Credits to order medicines, any healthcare products or book lab tests from Apollo 24|7.
Will some of these features be a part of the Apollo Hospital experience as well?
Not as of now, but we are working towards it so you get the best out of your CIRCLE membership.
Are CIRCLE benefits applicable on hospital visits booked through Apollo 24|7?
No, CIRCLE benefits are applicable only in cases of virtual consultation with doctors on the Apollo 24|7 app, but we are working towards it so you get the best out of your CIRCLE membership
What is the validity of Health Credits?
The standard validity of Health Credits is 1 year from the date of earning, unless otherwise specified. Health Credits earned by CIRCLE members via coupons are valid for 30 days.
How many Health Credits can I use against a transaction?
You can pay up to 50% of the transaction value via Health Credits.
Do we get a free delivery on all orders?
Delivery will be free for all orders above Rs.250.
